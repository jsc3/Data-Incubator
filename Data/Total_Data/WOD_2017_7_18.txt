Dallas 5
5 minutes of:
          Burpees
Then, 5 minutes of:
           7 deadlifts, 155 lb.
           7 box jumps, 24-in. box
Then, 5 minutes of:
           Turkish get-ups, 40-lb. dumbbell
Then, 5 minutes of:
           7 snatches, 75 lb.
           7 push-ups
Then, 5 minutes of:
          Rowing (calories)
Complete as many reps as possible at each 5-minute  station. Rest 1 minute between stations.
Post reps for each station to comments.
