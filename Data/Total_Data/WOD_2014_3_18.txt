Workout 14.2
Every 3 minutes for as long as possible complete:
From 0:00-3:00
   2 rounds of:
   95-lb. overhead squats, 10 reps
   10 chest-to-bar pull-ups
From 3:00-6:00
   2 rounds of:
   95-lb. overhead squats, 12 reps
   12 chest-to-bar pull-ups
From 6:00-9:00
   2 rounds of:
   95-lb. overhead squats, 14 reps
   14 chest-to-bar pull-ups
Etc., following same pattern until you fail to complete both rounds.
Post rounds and reps completed to comments and/or register and submit your results as part of the 2014 Reebok CrossFit Games Open.
Top male and female scores will receive $2,014.
